Strategies & how to implement

      
Each preset includes settings and an action checklist. Use on any platform—just mirror the indicators/settings.

      

        

          
EMA Touch (Green Line)

          
Trend-following. Alert only when price touches EMA (e.g., 9 or 7) in trend direction.

          
    
            
  •  Settings: EMA(9), EMA(50); timeframe: M5–H1
    • 
            
  •  Entry: touch EMA in trend direction; avoid consolidation
    • 
            
  •  Great for OTC/PocketOptions (one alert per candle)
    • 
          

        

        

          
RSI + MACD Momentum

          
Capture momentum bursts when RSI crosses midline with MACD expansion.

          
    
            
  •  RSI(14) > 50 for longs; MACD histogram rising
    • 
            
  •  Stops at prior swing; targets at R multiples
    • 
          

        

        

          
Stochastic + Williams %R

          
Confluence entries: Stoch blue over orange & W%R up for BUY (inverse for SELL).

          
    
            
  •  Stoch(7,4,3), W%R(7); confirm EMA(50) trend
    • 
            
  •  Avoid overextended reads without pullback
    • 
          

        

        

          
Break of Structure (BOS)

          
Detects structure breaks + retests for precision continuation entries.

          
    
            
  •  Long: HH + HL then break above last high + retest
    • 
            
  •  Confluence with RSI(50) + volume expansion
    • 
          

        

        

          
Opening Range Breakout (ORB)

          
Monitors first 15-minute range and alerts break direction.

          
    
            
  •  Assets: EUR/USD, GBP/JPY, NAS100, SPX500, US30, metals/commodities
    • 
            
  •  Stop: other side of the range; Targets: 1R–2R
    • 
          

        

        

          
Support / Resistance

          
Find reaction zones; wait for rejection/confirmation; enter with risk-defined levels.

          
    
            
  •  Confluence with EMA trend or RSI midline
    • 
            
  •  Scale out at the first trouble area
    •
