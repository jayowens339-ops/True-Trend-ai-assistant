How it works

TrueTrend AI reads live market data, computes technical context (trend, momentum, levels), and returns a clean bias (long/short/neutral). It logs “why you won/lost” so you improve faster. No copy-trading—this is your edge, not someone else’s.

Strategies (built-in)

  • Trend-SMA 20/50 — Baseline engine: 20/50 SMA + RSI filter.
  • Break of Structure — HH/HL & LL/LH with volume to flag regime changes.
  • Support/Resistance — Auto swing levels + ATR buffers.
  • ORB — Opening Range Breakout with momentum.
  • Portfolio Mode — Sector trends & ETF rotation signals.
