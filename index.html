How it works

TrueTrend AI reads live market data, computes technical context (trend, momentum, levels), and returns a clean bias (long/short/neutral) with confidence. It logs “why you won/lost” so you improve faster. No copy-trading—this is your edge, not someone else’s.

Strategies (built-in)

  • Trend-SMA 20/50 — Bias from 20/50 SMA slope & cross + RSI filter.
  • Break of Structure — HH/LL with momentum to flag regime changes.
  • Support/Resistance — Auto key levels from recent swings (ATR buffer).
  • ORB — Opening-range breakout + momentum.
  • Portfolio Mode — Sector momentum & ETF rotation signals.
